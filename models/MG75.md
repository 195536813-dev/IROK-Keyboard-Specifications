# IROK MG75 Full Aluminum Magnetic Keyboard Series

The MG75 series features a popular 75% (81-key) layout, combining a premium CNC aluminum chassis with IROK's most advanced magnetic switch technology for professional-grade gaming.

---

## 1. MG75 Max (Wired High-Performance)
A high-precision magnetic keyboard featuring the 2nd Gen G Turbo algorithm and a quick-release Gasket structure.

### 🛠 Technical Specifications
- **Performance & Accuracy:**
  - **RT Accuracy:** **0.001mm**
  - **Step Precision:** 0.001mm
  - **Polling Rate:** 8000Hz (8K)
  - **Latency:** 0.08ms
  - **Scanning Rate:** 256KHz Single-key / 32KHz Full-key
  - **Algorithm:** 2nd Gen G Turbo (Dynamic Balance & Real-time Temperature Correction)
- **Hardware Build:**
  - **Layout:** 75% (81 Keys)
  - **Case Material:** CNC Full Aluminum Alloy
  - **Structure:** Quick-release Design | Esports Gasket Mount
  - **Switch:** Qinghui Magnetic Switch
  - **Plate:** Aluminum Alloy
  - **Keycaps:** PC Frosted (Transparent)
  - **Protection:** PCB Nano Waterproofing
- **Lighting & Software:**
  - **Backlight:** ARGB (Full-range Spacebar with 3 dedicated LEDs)
  - **Driver:** Web Driver & HUB Support
  - **Functions:** KME, Music Rhythm, TGL, MT, Macro
- **Case Finish:** Anodized (Silver, Black, Red) | Electrophoresis (White, Pink)

---

## 2. MG75 V2 (Flagship Dual-MCU)
The ultimate evolution of the MG75, featuring the **StarSpin Dual-MCU architecture** and **IP67 rated protection**.

### 🛠 Technical Specifications
- **Ultimate Performance:**
  - **Architecture:** **StarSpin Dual-MCU Full-Chain Direct Link**
  - **Latency:** **0.07ms (Industry Leading)**
  - **RT Accuracy:** **0.001mm (Full-range)**
  - **Polling Rate:** 8000Hz (8K)
  - **Scanning Rate:** 512K Full-key / 147500K Single-key
- **Hardware Build:**
  - **Layout:** 75% (81 Keys)
  - **Case Material:** CNC Full Aluminum Alloy
  - **Backplate:** Hammer Wave Pattern Design (锻波工艺)
  - **Structure:** Quick-release Design | Esports Gasket Mount
  - **Switch:** **Qinghui Magnetic Switch PRO**
  - **Plate:** Aluminum Alloy
  - **Internal Padding:** PO Plate Foam + PCB Film + EPDM Case Foam + PET Gasket
  - **Keycaps:** PC Frosted (Transparent)
- **Durability & Features:**
  - **Protection:** **IP67 Rated PCB Nano Waterproofing**
  - **Lighting:** ARGB
  - **Functions:** **SOCD (Snap Tap)**, Music Rhythm, TGL, MT, Macro, MPT, END, DKS
- **Case Finish:** Anodized (Silver, Black, Red)

## 🚀 Key Advantages of the MG75 Series

- **Quick-Release Design:** The innovative Gasket structure allows for easy maintenance and customization without complex tools.
- **StarSpin Architecture (V2 Only):** Provides the lowest input lag possible (0.07ms), giving players a competitive edge in FPS titles.
- **Precision Engineering:** With 0.001mm RT accuracy, these keyboards offer the most consistent Rapid Trigger performance in the industry.
- **OS Support:** Windows
