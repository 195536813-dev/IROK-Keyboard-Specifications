# IROK ND63 Magnetic Switch Keyboard Series

The ND63 series features a compact 60% (63-key) layout designed for maximum mouse space and elite-tier gaming performance. Equipped with IROK's 2nd Gen G Turbo algorithm and an 8000Hz polling rate, it is built for professional-level esports.

---

## 1. ND63 Max (High-Performance Edition)
A compact magnetic keyboard offering superior response and stability for competitive gaming.

### 🛠 Technical Specifications
- **Performance & Accuracy:**
  - **RT Accuracy:** **0.005mm**
  - **Polling Rate:** 8000Hz (8K)
  - **Latency:** 0.125ms
  - **Scanning Rate:** 16KHz Full-key
  - **Platform/Solution:** Rongyuan (容圆)
  - **Algorithm:** 2nd Gen G Turbo (Dynamic Balance & Real-time Temperature Correction)
- **Hardware Build:**
  - **Layout:** 63 Keys (Compact 60%)
  - **Connection:** Wired (High-speed USB-C)
  - **Switch:** Purple Star Magnetic Switch
  - **Plate:** Aluminum Alloy Positioning Plate
  - **Internal Padding:** Poron Plate Foam + Silicone Base Pad
  - **Keycaps:** PC Frosted (Transparent Texture)
- **Lighting & Software:**
  - **Backlight:** ARGB
  - **Driver:** Web Driver & HUB Support
  - **Functions:** **SOCD**, TGL, MT, Macro, MPT, END
- **Colors:** Black / White

## 🚀 Key Advantages of the ND63 Series

- **Ultra-Compact Design:** The 63-key layout is highly favored by FPS professionals for providing maximum desk space for large mouse movements.
- **StarFlash 480 Platform (Ultra Only):** Provides an unprecedented 0.08ms input lag, making it one of the fastest compact keyboards on the market.
- **Extreme Precision:** The 0.001mm RT accuracy allows for instantaneous movement stops and resets, critical for high-tier FPS gameplay.
- **Advanced Thermal Correction:** The 2nd Gen G Turbo algorithm prevents "magnetic drift" caused by temperature changes, ensuring consistent performance during long gaming sessions.
- **OS Support:** Windows
